What is Kurkure

Ingredients 

Kurkure is made from rice meal, edible vegetable oil[9] (palm oil), corn meal, gram meal, spices (such as
            turmeric), condiments, salt, sugar, tartaric, and E631. Kurkure is not completely vegan as the PepsiCo India
            website in a pdf listing the ingredients show milk solids as one of the ingredients.
